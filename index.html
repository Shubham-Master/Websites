The Process

How a Circle Works

Simple, structured, and safe. Every session follows a gentle flow designed to make opening up feel natural — even for first-timers.

1

Pick a Theme

Choose a session that resonates — from "Navigating the Workplace" to "Rediscovering Joy in Music."

2

Enter the Circle

Join a small, curated group of 5–7 strangers on our clarity-first platform — optional live captions and a facilitator-led flow keep conversation clear for everyone.

3

Tell Your Story

Share a 5-minute story, or simply listen. A trained facilitator ensures everyone has space to speak.

4

Feel Heard

Others reflect back — not advice, just empathy. You leave lighter than you arrived.

"The Ground Rules"
✓ No advice-giving (unless asked) ✓ No interrupting ✓ What's said here, stays here ✓ Pseudonyms welcome ✓ Camera optional
Clarity-First Design

Features That Make Conversation Clear for Everyone

Our tools are designed to help conversations land — for every listener and every speaker. Clarity-first features are optional and built for the whole group, not a specific condition.

💬

Real-Time Captions

Optional live transcription appears on screen to support comprehension and shared attention for everyone in the circle.

🔕

One-Speaker Flow

Facilitators keep a single-speaker structure so stories are heard fully, without cross-talk or overlapping audio.

🫡

No Interruptions

Respectful turn-taking creates a calm space where sharing feels safe and contained.

🔒

Zero-Recording Policy

Sessions are not recorded by default — no audio or video is stored. Your story stays in the room.

About Unmute

Built for Deeper Listening

Unmute began from a simple conviction: modern life needs more spaces for honest, undistracted storytelling. People are full of important stories — they just rarely get the room to tell them.

We are not a clinic or a therapy service. We are a curated community that believes storytelling builds confidence, connection, and clarity. Our method is structured empathy — deliberate prompts, respectful pacing, and experienced facilitation.

Every session is designed so the story is the focus: small groups, clear turn-taking, and a warm environment that makes sharing feel safe and meaningful.
