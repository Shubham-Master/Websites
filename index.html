About Unmute

Built from Personal Experience

Unmute was born from a simple but urgent observation: people with hearing loss are not lacking stories ‚Äî they're lacking spaces where their stories can be heard on their own terms.

We are not a clinic, a therapy service, or a disability support group. We are a creative community, built on the belief that storytelling is one of the most powerful tools for rebuilding confidence and human connection.

Every session is designed to flip the script ‚Äî instead of your hearing being the thing that defines the interaction, your story becomes the centrepiece.
